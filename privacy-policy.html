Live in Oz – Privacy Policy

Last updated: 30/11/2025

This Privacy Policy explains how Live in Oz ("Live in Oz", "we", "us", "our") collects, uses and protects your personal information when you use our website and related services.

This Policy applies to:

  • The Live in Oz website and all pages on it
  • The Marketplace (buy/sell items, services, rooms, jobs)
  • The Accommodation / Bedrooms section, including Verified Badges and ID upload
  • The Jobs & Services section
  • The WhatsApp groups directory
  • The Oz Chat web version accessed through the website

If you use the Oz Chat mobile apps, an additional Oz Chat Privacy Policy may apply. In case of conflict, the more specific policy for that feature or app will take priority.

By using our website or creating an account, you agree to the collection and use of information in accordance with this Privacy Policy.

Live in Oz is a community platform helping people in Australia connect for accommodation, jobs, services, social groups and chat.

2. Information we collect

We collect different types of information depending on how you use the website.

2.1. Information you provide directly

When you use our site, you may provide:

  • Account details – name, username, email address, password, profile photo, city.
  • Listings and posts – titles, descriptions, photos, prices, availability, location (suburb/area), and any other details you choose to include in accommodation, Marketplace, job or service listings.
  • Messages and communications – messages you send via the site (for example, between buyers and sellers, or in Oz Chat web), and communications you send to us (for example, support emails).
  • Verification information – copies of ID documents or similar information that you upload for accommodation Verified Badges or other verification processes.
  • Payment-related information – if we ever offer paid features, we may collect billing details (for example, transaction IDs). Payment card information is normally processed by third-party payment processors, not stored directly by us.
  • Other information you choose to share – feedback, reviews, survey responses and preferences.

2.2. Information we collect automatically

When you visit our website, we automatically collect certain information, including:

  • Log and usage data – IP address, browser type, device type, pages visited, time and date of visits, referring website, interactions with pages and buttons.
  • Cookies and similar technologies – small text files stored on your device to keep you logged in, remember your preferences, and help us understand how the site is used.
  • Approximate location – derived from your IP address, to show relevant content (for example, city-specific listings) and for security and fraud prevention.

2.3. Information from third parties

We may receive information about you from:

  • Social logins or single sign-on (if offered) – such as name and email address when you sign in using another platform.
  • Payment providers – limited information related to payments (amount, time, status).
  • Analytics and advertising partners – aggregated data about site usage and performance.

We do not buy lists of personal data from third parties.

3. How we use your information

We use your information for the following purposes:

  1. To provide and operate the website and services
    • Creating and managing your account
    • Displaying your listings, posts and profile to other users
    • Allowing you to contact other users and respond to messages
    • Facilitating use of Oz Chat web, Marketplace, accommodation, jobs, services and WhatsApp directory
  2. To verify identity and provide trusted listings
    • Reviewing ID documents to issue Verified Badges for accommodation listings
    • Reducing fraud, scams and fake profiles
  3. To communicate with you
    • Sending service messages (for example, account confirmation, security alerts, updates about important changes)
    • Responding to your questions and support requests
    • Sending optional marketing or community updates (you can opt out at any time)
  4. To keep the platform safe
    • Monitoring for, and acting on, content that may be spam, fraud, abuse or otherwise against our Terms
    • Investigating reports and complaints
    • Enforcing our Terms of Use and community rules
  5. To improve the website
    • Analysing how the site is used
    • Fixing bugs and technical issues
    • Developing new features and services
  6. To comply with legal obligations
    • Keeping records required by law
    • Responding to lawful requests from authorities or regulators where necessary.

Where applicable (for example under the GDPR), we rely on the following legal bases to process your personal information:

  • Performance of a contract – to provide you with the services you requested by creating an account and using the site.

  • Consent – for certain optional cookies, marketing communications, and ID verification where required.

We may receive limited information from:

2.4 To improve the App

  • Analyse how people use OzChat to understand which features work well and which need improvement.

2.5 To comply with legal obligations

  • Keep records required by law.
